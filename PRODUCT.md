# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Wedding guests in Indonesia, primarily on mobile devices, receiving a personalized link via WhatsApp. They open the invitation once, read through it in one session, and may RSVP or leave a wish. Most are family and close friends of the couple.

## Product Purpose

A digital wedding invitation that delivers an intimate, emotionally resonant experience for each guest. Success means the guest feels personally welcomed, absorbs the wedding details clearly, and optionally RSVPs or leaves a wish without friction.

## Brand Personality

Romantic, Intimate, Timeless. The design carries the weight of the occasion: a Muslim wedding ceremony in Indonesia. Sacred and celebratory at once.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with hero metrics and CTA buttons
- Overly animated, attention-grabbing interfaces that distract from the couple
- Cold, minimal designs that strip out warmth
- Western-only wedding aesthetics that ignore Islamic context

## Design Principles

1. **Warmth over spectacle.** Every interaction should feel personal, not performant. Motion serves the moment, not the interface.
2. **Mobile first, always.** Guests open this on their phones. Every section, animation, and image must read beautifully at 390px.
3. **Content is sacred.** Typography and imagery carry the most weight. Chrome and decoration support them; they never compete.
4. **Graceful reveal.** The invitation unfolds like a physical envelope: the splash is the envelope, the content is the letter inside.
5. **Inclusive by default.** WCAG AA contrast, reduced-motion respect, no content hidden behind broken transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast. Standard motion (most guests expect a refined animated experience on mobile). Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` with crossfade fallbacks. All interactive elements (lightbox nav, RSVP form) keyboard accessible.
